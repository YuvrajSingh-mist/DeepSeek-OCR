# Mixed Cations and Structural Complexity in \((\mathbf{Eu}_{1 - x}\mathbf{Ca}_x)_4\mathbf{In}_3\mathbf{Ge}_4\) and  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[205, 160, 825, 175]]<|/det|>
\(\mathbf{A}_{2[\mathbf{n} + \mathbf{m}]}\mathbf{In}_{2\mathbf{n} + \mathbf{m}}\mathbf{Ge}_{2[\mathbf{n} + \mathbf{m}]}\) (n, \(\mathbf{m} = 1\) , 2, \(\dots\) ; A: Ca, Sr, Ba, Eu, or Yb).  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[205, 175, 785, 191]]<|/det|>
\(\mathbf{Eu}_{1,23}\mathbf{Ca}_{2,77}\mathbf{In}_3\mathbf{Ge}_4\) (I), \(\mathbf{Eu}_{2,60}\mathbf{Ca}_{1,40}\mathbf{In}_3\mathbf{Ge}_4\) (II), \(\mathbf{Eu}_{0,30}\mathbf{Ca}_{2,70}\mathbf{In}_2\mathbf{Ge}_3\) (III), and  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[205, 191, 911, 338]]<|/det|>
\(\mathbf{Eu}_{0,66}\mathbf{Ca}_{2,34}\mathbf{In}_2\mathbf{Ge}_3\) (IV) are synthesized from the elements in a molten In metal flux (960 °C, 20 h). The compounds are characterized by single crystal XRD, magnetic susceptibility measurements, and TB- LMTO band structure calculations. (I) and (II) crystallize in the space group C2/m with Z = 2, and (III) and (IV) in the space group Pnma with Z = 4. The polyanionic substructures of the compounds are based on InGe4 edge- sharing tetrahedra, Ge2 dimers, and bridging In atoms in a nearly square planar environment. — (YOU, T.- S.; TOBASH, P.
