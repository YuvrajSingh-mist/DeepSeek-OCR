## Ethical Questions  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[305, 71, 847, 108]]<|/det|>
When you encounter an ethical decision and must choose a course of action, ask yourself these important questions:  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[321, 113, 866, 208]]<|/det|>
Is it against the law? Does it violate company or professional policies? Even if everyone is doing it, how would I feel if someone did this to me? Am I sacrificing long- term benefits for short- term gains?  

<|ref|>sub_title<|/ref|><|det|>[[305, 228, 750, 251]]<|/det|>
## The Ethical Decision-Making Process  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[305, 253, 839, 289]]<|/det|>
Here are some steps to take if you find yourself in an ethical dilemma:  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[325, 295, 714, 388]]<|/det|>
1. Identify the ethical dilemma.  
2. Discover alternative actions.  
3. Decide who might be affected.  
4. List the probable effects of the alternatives.  
5. Select the best alternative.  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[304, 396, 855, 452]]<|/det|>
Using this process will enable you to make a more informed ethical choice. Making an ethical decision involves more people than just you.
