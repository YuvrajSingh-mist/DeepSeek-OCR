## Internet Auction Fraud  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[170, 668, 761, 784]]<|/det|>
By far the most reported Internet- related offense is Internet auction fraud. According to the Internet Fraud Complaint Center, Internet auction fraud comprised over 46 percent of referred fraud complaints in 2002, nearly 8 percent more than the previous year. Nondelivery and payment comprised 31 percent of complaints and credit/debit card fraud were nearly 12 percent. (Source: IFCC 2002 Internet Fraud Report)
