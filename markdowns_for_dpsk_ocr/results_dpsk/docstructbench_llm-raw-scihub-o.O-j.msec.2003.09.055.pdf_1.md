## Abstract  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[68, 400, 916, 500]]<|/det|>
A new ionic conjugated polymer, poly(2- ethynyl- N- methylpyridinium iodide) (PEMPI), was synthesized by the direct polymerization of 2- ethynylpyridine and methyl iodide under mild reaction condition. Another similar conjugated polymer with different counter- ion, poly(2- ethynyl- N- methylpyridinium tetraphenylborate) (PEMTPPB) was also prepared by the ion- exchange reaction of poly(2- ethynyl- N- methylpyridinium iodide) using sodium tetraphenylborate. The polymer structures were characterized by various instrumental methods to have conjugated polymer system with pyridyl moieties of different counter- ion. The photoluminescence spectra of these polymers having different counter- anions indicated that there is no different inductive and size effects of counter- anion between iodide and tetraphenylborate anions in this conjugated polymer backbone.

## 1. Introduction  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[70, 610, 472, 716]]<|/det|>
Polyacetylene and its homologues have been studied as organic semiconductors [1- 3], membranes for gas separation and for liquid- mixture separation [4], chiro- optical materials [5,6], side- chain liquid crystals [7- 9], materials for chemical sensors [10], and materials for nonlinear optical property [11,12] and photoluminescence and electroluminescence properties [13- 17].  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[70, 717, 472, 881]]<|/det|>
Polyacetylenes with ionic nature have potential as materials for mixed ionic and electronic conductivities, energy storage devices such as batteries and permselective membrane, and light- emitting devices due to the their extensive conjugation and various functionalities [2,18]. The synthesis of simple mono- and disubstituted ionic polyacetylenes from ethynylpyridines had been carried out with the activation of the acetylene bond in ethynylpyridines [18,19]. We have also studied on the synthesis and characterization of the poly(2- ethynylpyridine)- based conjugated polymers having various functionalities [2,9,14].  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[510, 581, 914, 641]]<|/det|>
In this paper, we report the synthesis of novel ionic conjugated polymers having different counter- anion (iodide, tetraphenylborate) and the characterization of resulting ionic conjugated polymers.

## 2. Experimental  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[510, 702, 914, 927]]<|/det|>
The conjugated ionic polymer, poly(2- ethynyl- N- methylpyridinium iodide) (PEMPI) was prepared by the direct polymerization of 2- ethynylpyridine with methyl iodide without any initiator system in methanol solvent. The polymerization was performed at \(70^{\circ}\mathrm{C}\) after standing for \(1\mathrm{h}\) because of the low boiling temperature of methyl iodide. Another ionic polymer, poly(2- ethynyl- N- methylpyridinium tetraphenylborate) (PEMTPPB) was easily prepared by the ion- exchange reaction of PEMPI with sodium tetraphenylborate in methanol solvent. FT- IR spectra were obtained with a Bruker EQUINOX 55 spectrometer using a KBr pellet. NMR spectra were recorded on a Varian 500 MHz FT- NMR spectrometer (Model: Unity INOVA) in DMSO- \(\mathrm{d}_6\) .
