<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[125, 157, 556, 177]]<|/det|>
(1)1. 你想让别人和你一起擦窗户, 你应该说:  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[202, 190, 800, 240]]<|/det|>
A. Let me clean the classroom. 
B. Let's clean the windows. 
C. Let me clean the windows.  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[125, 254, 456, 273]]<|/det|>
(2)2. 你想自己擦桌子, 你应该说:  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[203, 286, 800, 336]]<|/det|>
A. Let me clean the chair. 
B. Let's clean the windows. 
C. Let me clean the desk.  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[125, 351, 476, 370]]<|/det|>
(3)3. 当你想帮助别人时, 你应该说:  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[203, 384, 717, 434]]<|/det|>
A. Let you help me. 
B. Please help me. 
C. Let me help you.  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[125, 448, 456, 467]]<|/det|>

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[203, 481, 678, 531]]<|/det|>
A. Excuse me. 
B. Thank you. 
C. I'm sorry.
