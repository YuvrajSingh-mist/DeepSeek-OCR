## Why Local First?  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[390, 156, 570, 180]]<|/det|>
1. Local First Engages. It creates and deepens relationships within a community, which becomes more vibrant through collaboration and mutual investment.  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[390, 185, 567, 214]]<|/det|>
2. Local First Humanities. Physical and economic development occur on a scale that nurtures people and encourages beauty in natural and manmade environments.  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[390, 220, 560, 244]]<|/det|>
3. Local First Preserves. When more citizens are genuine stakeholders, protecting local economic and environmental value is of mutual interest.  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[390, 250, 570, 280]]<|/det|>
4. Local First Strengthens. When citizens and local businesses work together, it catalyzes positive development and the community has structural integrity to withstand economic downturns.  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[390, 286, 570, 308]]<|/det|>
5. Local First Invests. Sustainable local economies and ecosystems grow into markets that are greater than the sum of their parts.  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[390, 314, 570, 344]]<|/det|>
6. Local First Encourages. Intimate connections between customers and business facilitate innovation, interaction, and discovery, making for a more vibrant and resilient local economy.  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[390, 350, 570, 374]]<|/det|>
7. Local First Enriches. Reinventing success in customers, employees, and the local environment makes the entire community wealthier.
