(2) 利弊型的议论文 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[100, 170, 930, 240]]<|/det|>
Nowadays, there is a widespread concern over (the issue that) 作文题目. In fact, there are both advantage and disadvantage in 题目议题. Generally speaking, it is widely believed there are several positive aspects as follows. Firstly, 优点一. And secondly 优点二. 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[100, 240, 930, 300]]<|/det|>
Just as a popular saying goes, "every coin has two sides." 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[100, 300, 930, 360]]<|/det|>
议论文题 is no exception, and in another word, it still has negative aspects. To begin with, 缺点一. In addition, 缺点二. 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[100, 360, 930, 512]]<|/det|>
To sum up, we should try to bring the advantage of 议论文题 into full play, and reduce the disadvantage to the minimum at the same time. In that case, we will definitely make a better use of the 议论文题.

四. 议论文模板 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[100, 545, 410, 576]]<|/det|>
1. 正反观点式议论文模板： 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[130, 576, 930, 639]]<|/det|>
导入：第一段：Recently we've had a discussion about whether we should... (导入话题) 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[190, 639, 835, 672]]<|/det|>
Our opinions are divided on this topic. (观点有分歧) 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[130, 670, 944, 732]]<|/det|>
正文：第二段：Most of the students are in favour of it. (正方观点)
Here are the reasons. First... Second... Finally... (列出2~3个赞成理由) 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[190, 732, 940, 826]]<|/det|>
第三段：However, the others are strongly against it. (反方观点)
Their reasons are as follows. In the first place... What's more...
In addition... (列出2~3个反对理由) 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[130, 826, 930, 920]]<|/det|>
结论：第四段：Personally speaking, the advantages outweigh the disadvantages, for it will do us more harm than good. So I support it. (个人观点).
