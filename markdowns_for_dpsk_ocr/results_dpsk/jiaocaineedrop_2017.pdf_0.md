1. 传输线特征阻抗为 \(\mathrm{Z}_{0}\) , 负载阻抗为 \(\mathrm{R}_{\mathrm{L}}\) , 且 \(\mathrm{Z}_{0} \neq \mathrm{R}_{\mathrm{L}}\) , 若用特性阻抗为 \(\mathrm{Z}_{01}\) 的 \(1 / 4\) 波长阻抗变换器进行匹配, 则 \(\mathrm{Z}_{01}\) 应满足条件 ( C ).  

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[140, 507, 670, 533]]<|/det|>
\[\mathrm{A.} Z_{01} = Z_{0}R_{L} \quad \mathrm{B.} Z_{0} = \sqrt{Z_{01}R_{L}} \quad \mathrm{C.} Z_{01} = \sqrt{Z_{0}R_{L}} \quad \mathrm{D.} Z_{01} = R_{L}\]  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[115, 543, 881, 592]]<|/det|>
2. 一圆极化波垂直投射于一理想导体平板上(平板和 \(z\) 轴垂直,位于 \(z = b\) ),入射电场 \(\mathbf{E} = E_{m}\left(\mathbf{x}_{0} + j\mathbf{y}_{0}\right)e^{-j k z}\) ,则反射波电场为( C )  

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[140, 601, 664, 624]]<|/det|>
\[\mathrm{A.} \mathbf{E} = E_{m}\left(\mathbf{x}_{0} + j\mathbf{y}_{0}\right)e^{-j k z} \qquad \mathrm{B.} \mathbf{E} = -E_{m}\left(\mathbf{x}_{0} + j\mathbf{y}_{0}\right)e^{j k(z - b)}\]  

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[140, 631, 636, 653]]<|/det|>
\[\mathrm{C.} \mathbf{E} = -E_{m}\left(\mathbf{x}_{0} + j\mathbf{y}_{0}\right)e^{j k(z - 2b)} \qquad \mathrm{D.} \mathbf{E} = -E_{m}\left(\mathbf{x}_{0} + j\mathbf{y}_{0}\right)e^{j k z}\]
