二、听录音，根据所听问句选答句。（10分）
（　　）1. A. Yes, he was.　　B. Yes, they are.　　C. No, they weren’t.
（　　）2. A. We have magic clothes.　　B. Clever people.　　C. Yes, they were clever.
（　　）3. A. Thank you.　　B. Of course.　　C. Look at my clothes.
（　　）4. A. No, they aren’t.　　B. Yes, it is.　　C. Yes, they fit me well.
（　　）5. A. I saw a big house.　　B. There was a big house.　　C. It’s a big house.
