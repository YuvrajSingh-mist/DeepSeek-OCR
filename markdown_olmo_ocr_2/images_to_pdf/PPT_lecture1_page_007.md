Matrix representation of system of linear equations

Given a system of linear equations
\[
\begin{cases}
x_1 - 2x_2 + x_3 = 0 \\
2x_2 - 8x_3 = 8 \\
-4x_1 + 5x_2 + 9x_3 = -9
\end{cases}
\]

The coefficient matrix is
\[
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & -2 & 1 \\
0 & 2 & -8 \\
-4 & 5 & 9
\end{bmatrix}
\]

The augmented matrix is
\[
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & -2 & 1 & 0 \\
0 & 2 & -8 & 8 \\
-4 & 5 & 9 & -9
\end{bmatrix}
\]
