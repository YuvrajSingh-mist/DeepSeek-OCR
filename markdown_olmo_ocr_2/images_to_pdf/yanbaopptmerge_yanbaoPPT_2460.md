奇点分类（III）

• \( \Delta = 0 \) A，有两个相同的特征值

\[
\mathbf{P}^{-1} \mathbf{A} \mathbf{P} = \begin{pmatrix} \lambda & \\ & \lambda \end{pmatrix}
\]

\[
\frac{du}{dt} = \lambda u \\
\frac{dv}{dt} = \lambda v
\]
\[
\begin{cases}
u = u_0 e^{\lambda t} \\
v = v_0 e^{\lambda t}
\end{cases}
\]
\[
\frac{u}{v} = \frac{u_0}{v_0}
\]

• \( \alpha < 0, \lambda < 0 \)
• \( \alpha > 0, \lambda > 0 \)

\[
\frac{d\mathbf{u}}{dt} = \mathbf{P}^{-1} \mathbf{A} \mathbf{P} \mathbf{u}
\]
\[
\alpha = \operatorname{tr}(\mathbf{A}) = \lambda_1 + \lambda_2
\]
\[
