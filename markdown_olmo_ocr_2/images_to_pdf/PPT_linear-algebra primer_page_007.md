Projection: Using Inner Products (I)

![Diagram showing projection of x along direction a](page_324_186_1017_591.png)

Projection of x along the direction \( \mathbf{a} \) (\( \| \mathbf{a} \| = 1 \)).

\[
\mathbf{p} = \mathbf{a} \left( \mathbf{a}^T \mathbf{x} \right)
\]
\[
\| \mathbf{a} \| = \mathbf{a}^T \mathbf{a} = 1
\]
