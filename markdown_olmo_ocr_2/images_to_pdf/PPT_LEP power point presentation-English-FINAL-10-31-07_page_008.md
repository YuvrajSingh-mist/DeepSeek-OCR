Department of Justice

Policy Guidance:
Enforcement of Title VI of the Civil Rights Act of 1964 *

DOJ recommends an analysis of four factors:

1. Identifying the number or proportion of LEP persons eligible to be served or likely to be encountered by the program or grantee
2. Frequency with which LEP individuals come in contact with the program
3. Nature and importance of the program, activity, or service
4. Resources available to the grantee/recipient and costs

* Initial guidance document published January 16, 2001; revised version went into effect June 12, 2002
