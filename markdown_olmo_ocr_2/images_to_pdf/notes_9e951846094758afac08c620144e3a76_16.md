7. 如图，梯形ABCD中：AB∥CD。若AB=1，CD=2，AC=6。S_{△AOB}=1。Rt|AO=______。
S_{梯形ABCD} = ______。

解：∵ AB∥CD
∴ △AOB∽△COD
即 \( \frac{AO}{CO} = \frac{AB}{CD} \) ∵ AB=1，CD=2，AC=6
∴ \( \frac{AO}{CO} = \frac{1}{2} \Rightarrow CO = 2AO \)

又∵ AC=AO+CO=3AO ⇒ 3AO=6，AO=2

又∵ \( \frac{S_{△AOB}}{S_{△COD}} = \left( \frac{AB}{CD} \right)^2 = \left( \frac{1}{2} \right)^2 = \frac{1}{4} = \frac{1}{S_{△COD}} \)
∴ S_{△COD} = 4

∵ S_{△AOB} 与 S_{△BOC}（同底，同高）
且底之比为 OA:OC = 1:2
∴ S_{△AOB} : S_{△BOC} = 1:2 ∵ S_{△AOB} = 1
∴ S_{△BOC} = 2 同理：S_{△AOD} 与 S_{△COD}（同底，同高）
且底之比为 OA:OC = 1:2 ∴ S_{△AOD} : S_{△COD} = 1:2
