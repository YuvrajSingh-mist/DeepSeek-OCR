Facts about our students

• Approximately 470 English majors.
• Roughly half to 2/3 are dual majors in Education.
• Roughly half of our majors enter as transfer students.
• Our majors have had great success in a wide variety of careers and in graduate school.
• Our majors score above state average on Praxis 2 in subject matter
• Two recent graduates earned a perfect score on Praxis.
• Three students have recently won Fulbright Awards, to South Korea (2007), Spain (2009), and South Africa (2010)
• One 2010 graduate earned a prestigious book contract for a memoir (Alice Ozma, The Reading Promise)
• See our Department Website (prospective student link) for information on careers for English Majors.
