KET写作 Part 9 专项训练
Grammar Prepositions

KET candidates often make mistakes describing where with at, on, in or to.
You might have to say where you are, where you did something, where something is or where you want to go.

Example   We went in a museum with our teacher.    ...to...

1 Your school bag is to the table in your room.    ...............
2 My classmates are at France now.    ....................
3 We went in a restaurant and had a curry.    ....................
4 My family arrived at New York yesterday.    ....................
5 You can buy the CD to the new music shop.    ....................
6 I was in a party at Jill's house.    .....................
