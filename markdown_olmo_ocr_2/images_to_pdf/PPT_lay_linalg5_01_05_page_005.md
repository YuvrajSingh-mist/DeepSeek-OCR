HOMOGENEOUS LINEAR SYSTEMS

- Solve for the basic variables \( x_1 \) and \( x_2 \) to obtain \( x_1 = \frac{4}{3} x_3 \), \( x_2 = 0 \), with \( x_3 \) free.
- As a vector, the general solution of \( Ax = 0 \) has the form given below.

\[
\mathbf{x} = \begin{bmatrix}
x_1 \\
x_2 \\
x_3
\end{bmatrix} =
\begin{bmatrix}
\frac{4}{3} x_3 \\
0 \\
x_3
\end{bmatrix} = x_3 \begin{bmatrix}
\frac{4}{3} \\
0 \\
1
\end{bmatrix} = x_3 \mathbf{v}, \text{ where } \mathbf{v} = \begin{bmatrix}
\frac{4}{3} \\
0 \\
1
\end{bmatrix}
\]
