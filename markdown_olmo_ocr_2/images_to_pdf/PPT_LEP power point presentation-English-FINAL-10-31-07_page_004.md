Executive Order No. 13166:
Improving Access to Services for Persons with Limited English Proficiency (LEP)

- August 11, 2000: President Bill Clinton issued the Executive Order 13166.
- Designed to improve access to federally conducted and federally assisted programs/activities for LEP persons
- Federal agencies were asked to develop and implement a system to provide LEP persons access to services.
- Each federal agency was asked to prepare a plan to improve access to LEP persons, and submit plans to the U.S. Department of Justice.
- Executive Order charged the Department of Justice with the responsibility of providing LEP guidance to federal agencies.
