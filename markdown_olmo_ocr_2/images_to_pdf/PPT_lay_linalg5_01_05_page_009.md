SOLUTIONS OF NONHOMOGENEOUS SYSTEMS

Solution: Row operations on \([A\ 0]\) produce

\[
\begin{bmatrix}
3 & 5 & -4 & 7 \\
-3 & -2 & 4 & -1 \\
6 & 1 & -8 & -4
\end{bmatrix}
\sim
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 0 & -\frac{4}{3} & -1 \\
0 & 1 & 0 & 2 \\
0 & 0 & 0 & 0
\end{bmatrix},
\]
\[
x_1 - \frac{4}{3} x_3 = -1 \\
x_2 = 2 \\
0 = 0
\]

Thus \(x_1 = -1 + \frac{4}{3} x_3\), \(x_2 = 2\), and \(x_3\) is free.
